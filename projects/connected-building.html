CONNECTED BUILDING
DESIGN LANGUAGE

THE PROBLEM

Honeywell's connected building software, although following the Honeywell Digital design language, needed greater consistency between software products. As some users might access several different Honeywell products, they needed to have a consistent placement and behavior for their controls and they needed to be united by a familiar style.

MY ROLE

We conducted a discovery workshop for design and business in order to agree on a set of needs and requirements for creating a connected building platform. I created initial visual design explorations for hero screens in order to set a tone for the look and feel of the digital experience and collaborated with other designers to extend and develop the final design language.

Initial Design Direction: Dark theme

IMPACT

The final design language was leveraged by studios worldwide and created into a development platform in order to increase Productivity and Efficiency across design and development teams, allowing greater speed to market.
