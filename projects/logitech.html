LOGITECH HARMONY ONE

THE PROBLEM

Logitech approached projekt202 to help them to create an intuitive user experience for their Harmony One Universal remote.

MY ROLE

Working within a tight screen real estate and restrictive hardware constraints, I did a series of design explorations for the UI of the Logitech’s high-end universal remote device touch screen UI and created different custom themes and activity-based screens.

IMPACT

While I can’t take credit for its eventual popularity, the Harmony One has since become one of the most successful remote controls in the world, with over 2 million units shipped.
